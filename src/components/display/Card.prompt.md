**Card** — the universal surface container; everything sits on one.

```jsx
<Card><h3 className="mk-headline-md">Waiting Queue</h3>…</Card>
<Card variant="glass" accent>Quick Summary…</Card>
<Card variant="ink">Patient Context (AI)…</Card>          {/* dark blue panel */}
<Card interactive accent>Up next — Sarah Montgomery</Card>  {/* hover lift */}
```

Variants: `tactile` (default raised), `flat`, `glass` (frosted, over imagery/dashboards), `ink` (dark brand blue with white text — AI & teleconsult promos), `sunken`. Add `interactive` for hover lift, `accent` for the teal left border.
